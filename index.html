Safety Takes Preparation

Consulting and Advisory Services For the Energy Industry

We can help you be prepared for challenges associated with producing oil and gas safely, in compliance with all regulations, and in an environmentally responsible manner. Safe operations and prevention of oil spills are always the top priorities. Preparation, detailed plans, training, drills, and execution are critical components for successful emergency response. Emergencies can occur from a diverse group of incidents such as: fires, explosions, hurricanes, earthquakes, floods, oil spills, security events, and many others. Emergencies can also become a crisis for a company which requires senior management involvement. With over 42 years of experience in the industry and over 25 years managing operations, emergencies, and crisis in the U.S. and many other countries, we can provide you with sound advice, processes, and procedures before, during, or after an emergency and/or crisis.
