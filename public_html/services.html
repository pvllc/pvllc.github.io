Safety Takes Preparation

Experience

Peter Velez has more than 35 years experience in a variety of leadership positions. As the Incident Commander for Shell, he has responded to incidents in the Gulf of Mexico, U.S. Onshore, and Global locations involving oil spills, hurricanes, fires and explosions, Y2K, and other events. He has performed work for Shell at locations in Alaska, Gulf of Mexico, Nigeria, Oman, Algeria, UAE, Gabon, UK, Norway, Netherlands, Russia, China, India, Mexico, Canada, Brazil, Bolivia, and Venezuela.

In addition to his work at Shell, he has led and participated in numerous energy industry and engineering professional groups; given speeches, presentations, and expert testimony before courts and legislatures. His extensive work has earned him numerous awards and recognitions from the US Coast Gaurd, the API, and others.
